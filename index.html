About Me

I am a mom of two wonderful kids and one cool pup and constantly looking for ways to simplify my life. I am working toward gaining a remote job to avoid commutes and achieve work-life balance, especially during Alaskan winters when many people take off work. While in Utah, I attended a People Helping People job fair at which I discovered the Tech Moms' table. Robbyn Scribner was my first point of contact; her enthusiasm got my attention. I am happy I stopped by the table that day. I am embarking on this tech journey thanks to the opportunity and empowerment provided by Tech Moms. Once a Tech Mom always a Tech Mom.











About Alaska

Alaska is a vast state, with a smalltown feel. Nature abounds. I love the peace and quiet that provides. Peace and quiet is priceless. Exploring what nature offers here is a must, but safety is paramount. The land of the extremes; I have experienced colder than "minus 70" (-70) degrees and over 100 degrees Fahrenheit. Winters are long and summers are short. Winter is a time for resting and preparation. Summer is a time of endless sun and GO GO GO. Denali National Park and Preserve is one place I like to visit. Adventure awaits.
